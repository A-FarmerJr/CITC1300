Inspiration for "EVANESCENT"

EVANESCENT is the working title for the my next film project, with a creative focus on color grading & editing.

What is EVANESCENT about?

EVANESCENT is a revenge story about a terminally ill husband, who in what he believes to be his final stretch of life, is given an extension the day after his wife is murdered in a robbery. The film will tackle the complexity of revenge as the main character acts ruthlessly to avenge his lover.

What is the setting of EVANESCENT?

The world of EVANESCENT is an early 2000s metropolis.
