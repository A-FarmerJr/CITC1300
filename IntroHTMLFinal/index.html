About the Game

*Life is Strange* is an episodic narrative adventure game developed by Dontnod Entertainment and published by Square Enix, first released in January 2015. The game centers on Max Caulfield, an 18-year-old photography student who discovers she can rewind time after witnessing a traumatic event at her school, Blackwell Academy. Set in the fictional coastal town of Arcadia Bay, Oregon, the game spans five episodes, each delving deeper into themes of friendship, grief, identity, and the moral complexities of choice. Max’s ability to alter the past creates a butterfly effect, where every decision impacts the lives of those around her, including her rebellious best friend, Chloe Price. The game’s hand-painted art style, emotional storytelling, and licensed indie soundtrack make it a standout in the adventure genre, earning critical acclaim for its narrative depth and character development. It’s a poignant exploration of adolescence and the power of human connection.
